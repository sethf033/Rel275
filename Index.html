Families Can Be Together Forever

What Does This Mean?

It means that God’s plan of happiness is centered on families—and that through Jesus Christ, those family relationships can continue beyond death. The temple in the picture represents a sacred place where families can be united forever through special covenants and ordinances. As the Church teaches, “Because of the temple, we can be together forever with the people we love most.” (churchofjesuschrist.org)

Knowing this truth brings peace and hope—it reminds us that family love is eternal and that God’s greatest desire is for all His children to return to Him and live together in joy as eternal families.

How Can I Know For Myself?

You can come to know for yourself that families can truly be together forever through sincere prayer, study, and living the teachings of Jesus Christ. The Holy Ghost can confirm truth to your heart and mind as you seek it. The Church teaches, “If we sincerely ask Heavenly Father for guidance, He will answer us through feelings, thoughts, and impressions from the Holy Ghost.” (churchofjesuschrist.org)

By reading the scriptures, attending church, and visiting the temple, you can feel God’s love and gain your own personal testimony that His plan is real—and that family relationships can continue beyond this life.
